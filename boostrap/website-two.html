The ITI Fitter course is a two-year mechanical trade course that teaches students about fitting. The course covers topics like basic safety, marking out, filling, heat tempering, lathe work, drilling, grinding, and different types of bolts and fittings.

The welder trade is a vocational training program that teaches the skills and knowledge needed to become a welder. The program is offered at many ITIs across India under the Craftsman Training Scheme (CTS) of the Directorate General of Training (DGT). The course is one year long.
